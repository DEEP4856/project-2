About Us

Tamizh Pasanga Events

Tamizh Pasanga Events is a locally known Photography Studio and Event Management company with work that has been featured in a large number of publications. We place an equal focus on the overall experience our clients have and on creating the perfect result. We do this to ensure that our clients’ needs are completely met while producing photos of the highest artistic quality.
Founded in 2018, Tamizh Pasanga Events began our photography journey shooting friends and family, and now offer custom-made, artistic photographic solutions to all people
